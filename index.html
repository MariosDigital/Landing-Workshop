About Us

At Mario's Digital we specialize in the repair of electronic devices, televisions, electric scooters and video game consoles. With over 10 years of experience, our commitment is to provide reliable solutions, fair prices and a personalized service that makes us stand out. Every repair is handled with dedication and professionalism, ensuring that your devices return to their best performance.

Our Services

Computer & Laptop Repair

Hardware and software diagnostics, component replacement, virus removal and complete optimization for desktops and laptops.

Television Repair

Repair of LED, LCD and Smart TVs: image problems, sound failures, motherboard replacements and general maintenance.

Electric Scooter Service

Battery changes, motor diagnostics, brake adjustments, tire replacement and preventive check-ups for all brands.

Videogame Consoles

Repair and maintenance of PlayStation, Xbox and Nintendo consoles: HDMI ports, overheating, system updates and cleaning.
